About TVM

Bridging the gap between law, business, and entertainment.

The Vie Mentorship was created for young lawyers who want to understand entertainment beyond legal theory. It connects emerging talent with practical industry knowledge, commercial perspective, and the guidance needed to serve creatives with confidence.

3

Years of building a clearer pathway for emerging entertainment lawyers.

Why We Started

A practical response to a real gap.

The mentorship program was created to bridge the gap between established professionals in Nigeria's entertainment industry and the next generation of entertainment lawyers. Many young lawyers entering the space have limited access to practical insight into how the industry truly works.

Without proper guidance, young lawyers are often left to learn by trial and error. Self-learning matters, but in entertainment law, avoidable mistakes can affect both lawyers and the creatives or businesses they represent.

TVM gives aspiring entertainment lawyers a stronger foundation, covering contracts, legal frameworks, industry stakeholders, business models, and the realities of working with artists, producers, labels, distributors, and creative companies.

Our goal is to equip young lawyers with the knowledge, perspective, and confidence to build meaningful careers while delivering greater value to the creative ecosystem.
